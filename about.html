Our Story

Crafting timeless elegance since 2023

Redefining Modern Elegance

BK Threads was founded on the principle that luxury clothing should be both beautiful and responsible. We believe in creating pieces that stand the test of time - in both style and durability.

The Beginning

Our journey began in a small atelier in New York, where founder Benjamin Kingsley set out to create suits that combined traditional tailoring techniques with contemporary design. What started as a passion project quickly grew into a movement as clients responded to our commitment to quality and sustainability.

Today, while we've grown beyond that original workshop, each BK Threads garment still receives the same meticulous attention to detail that defined our first collections.

Our Mission

At BK Threads, we're committed to:

  • Sustainable Practices

    Using eco-friendly materials and ethical production methods to minimize our environmental impact.

  • Timeless Design

    Creating pieces that transcend seasonal trends and become wardrobe staples.

  • Ethical Partnerships

    Working with artisans and suppliers who share our values of fair wages and safe working conditions.
